Welcome to THE PRAIRIE!

THE PRAIRIE is designed with your family's comfort in mind. These homes come with 1, 2, 10-year Warranty coverage.

Each of our beautiful, newly constructed houses features fenced-in backyards, providing both privacy and security. With spacious layouts, open kitchens, and expansive living areas, there's ample room for unforgettable family gatherings and cherished memories. Each home includes new appliances ensuring both convenience and sustainability for generations to come.

The Prairie Subdivision boasts homes designed to evoke pride and timeless charm. As you stroll through our well-lit streets, enjoy the peace of mind provided by our secluded public entrance. The Prairie is a community where families can walk their pets, ride their bikes, or play a fun game of kickball on a lazy Sunday afternoon.
