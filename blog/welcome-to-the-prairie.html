Welcome to The Prairie: Your New Home in Smithville

October 14, 2025 • 5 min read

Are you searching for the perfect place to call home? Somewhere that combines small-town charm with modern amenities, excellent schools with outdoor adventures, and community spirit with convenience? Welcome to The Prairie Smithville.

Why Families Choose The Prairie

The Prairie Smithville isn't just a neighborhood—it's a community where families flourish. Our development of 28 new construction homes brings together everything you've been looking for in one beautiful location in the heart of Central Texas.

Quality New Construction

Every home at The Prairie is built with quality and craftsmanship in mind. From the moment you walk through the door, you'll notice the difference:

  • Modern, open-concept floor plans perfect for family living
  • 2-4 bedrooms with 1,402-1,780 square feet of thoughtfully designed space
  • Energy-efficient features that save you money
  • Quality finishes throughout, including granite countertops and stainless steel appliances
  • Fenced backyards ready for kids, pets, and outdoor entertaining
  • 1-2-10 year warranty for complete peace of mind

The Smithville Advantage

What makes Smithville special? It's a town that has successfully preserved its historic charm while embracing modern growth. Founded in 1827, Smithville has a rich history—including being the filming location for beloved movies like "Hope Floats" and "Tree of Life."

But Smithville is more than its Hollywood connections:

  • Excellent Schools: Smithville ISD provides quality education with small class sizes and dedicated teachers
  • Perfect Location: Just 45 minutes from Austin, giving you access to big-city opportunities while enjoying small-town living
  • Outdoor Recreation: The Colorado River, parks, and trails offer endless opportunities for family adventures
  • Safe Community: Low crime rates and friendly neighbors create a secure environment for raising a family
  • Affordable Living: Get more home for your money compared to Austin and surrounding areas

Life at The Prairie

Imagine starting your mornings with a cup of coffee on your back porch, kids playing safely in your fenced yard. Picture evenings gathering around the kitchen island, homework spread out while dinner simmers on the stove. Weekend adventures might take you to the Colorado River for fishing, downtown Smithville for the farmers market, or just a short drive to Austin for shopping and entertainment.

Community Amenities

The Prairie neighborhood is designed with community in mind. Wide streets perfect for evening walks, sidewalks for kids learning to ride bikes, and neighbors who quickly become friends. You're also just minutes from:

  • Smithville's historic downtown with local shops and restaurants
  • Parks and recreational facilities
  • Schools and childcare options
  • Grocery stores and essential services
  • Medical facilities and pharmacies

Your New Beginning Starts Here

Whether you're a first-time homebuyer, growing family, or looking to downsize without sacrificing quality, The Prairie Smithville offers the perfect solution. Our move-in ready homes mean you can start making memories right away—no waiting for construction to finish, no unexpected delays.

Affordable New Construction

With homes starting at $316,500, The Prairie makes new construction accessible. You're not just buying a house; you're investing in:

  • A proven builder with a reputation for quality
  • Modern construction meeting the latest building codes
  • Energy efficiency that lowers your utility bills
  • Warranty protection for years to come
  • A neighborhood designed for the future

Visit The Prairie Today

We know choosing a home is one of life's biggest decisions. That's why we invite you to come see The Prairie for yourself. Walk through our available homes, explore the neighborhood, and imagine your family's future here.

Our friendly sales team is ready to answer your questions, explain the homebuying process, and help you find the perfect home for your family. Whether you're ready to buy now or just beginning your search, we're here to help every step of the way.
